About:

Jordynn is a passionate woman who thrives off of new knowledge and seeks to be successful in all aspects of life. Growing up competitively dancing, she learned how to manage stress, time, and work at a young age. This work ethic took her to Loyola Marymount University in Los Angeles, where she graduated Cum Laude with a BA in dance and minor in business administration.

Post graduation, she focused on pursing dance, fitness and photogrpahy in Los Angeles until she found herself stagnant and not challenged mentally. Jordynn moved home to Seattle, WA to further her skills and pursue a different career - web development! Currently, she teaches a fitness class, choreograph for dance studios, manage a social media account for a dance photography company, and is enrolled in a coding bootcamp.

Her experience in the arts marries the coding tools I have learned giving me the perfect amount of cre)tivity in web development. I am sure I will bring a new, fresh mind to the work place and look forward to always facing and fighting challenges.
