About

I am a passionate woman who thrives off of new knowledge and seeks to be successful in all aspects of life. Growing up competitively dancing, I learned how to manage stress, time, and work at a young age. This work ethic took me to Loyola Marymount University in Los Angeles, where I graduated Cum Laude with a BA in dance and minor in business administration.

Post graduation, I focused on pursing dance, fitness and photography in Los Angeles until I found myself stagnant and not challenged mentally. I moved home to Seattle, WA to further my skills and venture into a different career - web development! My experience in the arts marries the coding tools I have learned which gives me ample creativity in web development. I am confident in bringing a new, fresh mind to coding whether it is in the work place or for personal clients.
