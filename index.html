About

Jordynn Otto is a Seattle born and Los Angeles bred woman of many talents. Currently, she is focused on her career in front-end web development. Her clients span across colleagues, friends and referrals and she prides herself on turning the client's wants into a fulfilled cohesive application. Jordynn has a unique and fresh eye for design which marries her perfectionist and detailed-oriented personality eloquently. Communicating with others is joyous for Jordynn due to her bubbly, outgoing sense of self.

Post cum laude college graduation from Loyola Marymount University, Jordynn lived and worked in Los Angeles as a professional dancer. Her career experience includes, but is not limited to, dancing for Disney Channel, foregin hip-hop artists, local jazz clubs, commercials and feature films. Even though her career was booming, Jordynn strongly believes in maximizing her potential and she soon learned how to shoot dance photography and teach Pure Barre, a group fitness class for adults. By 2018, she felt like her professional career was missing a mental push, somethign to challenge her intellectually every day. So, in pure Jordynn demeanor, she moved back home to Seattle and enrolled, and completed, in a full-stack web development course.



"How you do anything, is how you do everything."



Jordynn would be an irreplacable asset to any front-end engineering team with her combined development tools, artistic skills and communication expertise. Focusing on detail, yet being a flexible and easy group memeber sets her apart from other developers due to her immense imersion in the arts. Jordynn is passionate about everything she does and tends to go above and beyound what is asked of her. From dancer, photographer, fitness instructor, and now software enginner, let Jordynn take care of any of your devlopement needs.
